# Filtering

**Posts Page**  
You can filter posts by **title**, **description**, **date**, and **tags** on the [Posts](/posts) page.

![Filtering UI](assets/filtering-ui.png)

**Command Menu**  
You can filter posts by **title** inside the command menu.
